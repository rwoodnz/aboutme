Wellington's Dev Academy is training people to be software developers with a 9-week intensive course licenced from San Francisco's Dev Bootcamp. The course is also preceded by weeks of part-time preparation so everyone is ready to hit the ground running on course. The group I'm with will be finished mid-July and ready-to-roll.
